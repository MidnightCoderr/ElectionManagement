## Before You Vote

### Requirements

✅ **Valid Aadhar Card** - Bring your 12-digit Aadhar number  
✅ **Registered Fingerprint** - Ensure you're registered in the system  
✅ **Valid Election** - Check that the election is currently active  

### Registration

If you haven't registered:

1. Visit your local election office
2. Provide your Aadhar card
3. Complete biometric registration (fingerprint scan)
4. Receive confirmation via SMS/Email

## Voting Process

### Step 1: Arrive at Polling Station

1. Find your assigned polling station
2. Look for the IoT voting terminal
3. Wait in queue if necessary

### Step 2: Biometric Authentication

<img src="images/fingerprint-scan.png" alt="Fingerprint Scanner" width="300"/>

1. **Place your finger** on the fingerprint scanner
2. **Hold steady** for 2-3 seconds
3. **Wait for confirmation**
   - ✅ **Green light** = Authentication successful
   - ❌ **Red light** = Try again

> **Note**: If authentication fails 3 times, consult a poll worker

### Step 3: View Candidates

<img src="images/candidate-list.png" alt="Candidate List" width="400"/>

1. Review all candidates on the screen
2. Use the **search bar** to find specific candidates
3. Read candidate information carefully

### Step 4: Select Your Candidate

1. **Tap** on your chosen candidate
2. Review your selection
3. Candidate card will be **highlighted**

### Step 5: Confirm Your Vote

<img src="images/confirm-vote.png" alt="Confirm Vote" width="400"/>

1. Verify your selection is correct
2. Read the confirmation message
3. Click **"Confirm Vote"** button

> ⚠️ **Warning**: This action cannot be undone!

### Step 6: Receive Receipt

<img src="images/vote-receipt.png" alt="Vote Receipt" width="350"/>

You will receive a **digital receipt** containing:

- ✅ Vote ID
- ✅ Timestamp
- ✅ Blockchain Transaction ID
- ✅ QR Code for verification

**Save this receipt!** You can use it to verify your vote was counted.

## Verifying Your Vote

### Online Verification

1. Visit: `https://verify.election-system.com`
2. Enter your **Vote ID** or scan the **QR code**
3. View confirmation that your vote was recorded

### What You'll See:

```
✅ Vote Recorded
Vote ID: VT-2024-ABC123
Timestamp: 2024-03-15 14:30:22
Status: CONFIRMED ON BLOCKCHAIN
Block: 12345
Transaction: 0xabc...def
```

> **Privacy Note**: The verification system confirms your vote was counted but does NOT reveal who you voted for.

## FAQ

### Q: Can I vote multiple times?
**A:** No. The system uses blockchain technology to prevent double voting. Once you vote, you cannot vote again in the same election.

### Q: What if my fingerprint doesn't work?
**A:** 
1. Clean your finger
2. Try a different finger
3. Consult a poll worker for manual verification

### Q: Is my vote anonymous?
**A:** Yes. Your vote is encrypted and stored separately from your identity. Even administrators cannot see who you voted for.

### Q: What if I accidentally vote for the wrong candidate?
**A:** Once you click "Confirm," the vote is final and cannot be changed. Please review carefully before confirming.

### Q: Can I see election results immediately?
**A:** Results are only visible after the election officially ends. Check the Observer Dashboard after the election closes.

### Q: What happens if the power goes out?
**A:** IoT terminals have battery backup and offline mode. Your vote will be stored locally and submitted when connectivity is restored.

### Q: How secure is this system?
**A:** The system uses:
- 🔒 Biometric authentication
- 🔗 Blockchain technology (immutable records)
- 🔐 End-to-end encryption
- 🛡️ ML fraud detection
- 📝 Comprehensive audit logs

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Biometric Authentication Failed"

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure finger is clean and dry
2. Press firmly but don't move
3. Try a different finger
4. Ask poll worker for assistance

### Problem: "You have already voted"

**Explanation:** You have already cast your vote in this election. The system prevents double voting for security.

**Action:** If this is an error, contact an election official immediately.

### Problem: "Election not active"

**Explanation:** The election has not started or has already ended.

**Action:** Check election dates and times. Voting is only allowed during the official voting period.

### Problem: "Terminal Offline"

**Explanation:** The voting terminal has lost internet connectivity.

**Action:** 
- Your vote will be stored locally
- It will automatically sync when connection is restored
- You can still receive a receipt

### Problem: "Invalid Vote Receipt"

**Explanation:** 
- QR code may be damaged
- Vote ID entered incorrectly

**Action:**
- Re-enter Vote ID carefully
- Scan QR code again
- Contact support if issue persists
